Help & Instructions

This page allows you to track your bets and manage them easily. Here's a detailed guide:

  • Filters: Use the date, result, and sport filters to narrow down your bet history.
  • Bet Cards: Each card shows:
    • Stake: The amount placed on the bet.
    • Odds: The odds for the bet.
    • Legs: Number of selections in the bet.
    • Earnings: Won/lost amount. Green: positive, Red: negative, Blue: cash out.
  • Delete Bet: Click × to remove a bet. A confirmation will appear.
